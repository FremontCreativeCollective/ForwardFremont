Why

Rich in history, the May Brothers Wholesale Grocers building at the corner of 6th and Park Ave had its roots in the entrepreneurial spirit. Back in the 1860's, brothers Joseph, Jacob and William May started a small grocery business on this site and later developed it into a regional grocery wholesaler.

Forward Fremont wants to tap into that same entrepreneurial spirit and create an environment where others can take their creative ideas, cultivate them and grow them into other regional or global solutions. The May brothers started the commotion within these walls many years ago and it is our goal to fire it back up and carry it on.

Fremont is in great need of a community to help inspire, encourage and motivate its citizens to release their creative dreams, helping them to become the best they can be while advancing Fremont at the same time.
