Frequently Asked Questions

The Forward Fremont project is planning on making things happen in Fremont that have never happened before. We expect our fair share of questions. Anytime we encounter a new one, we will add it to this list in an effort to better educate Fremont about what we're doing.

What building is Forward Fremont looking to purchase?

Forward Fremont is looking at acquiring the old May Brothers Groccers located on the south-east corner of 6th and Park. The building has served a number of purposes over the years but most recently served as the campaign headquarters for Fremont-native Ben Sasse's campaign headquarters.

Why Fremont?

Forward Fremont wants to see more technology companies take root in Fremont. We've seen great atmospheres and cultures created for technology companies in big cities like Omaha and Des Moines. However, we like the small town life and hate commuting. We would much rather see those types of cultures migrate to Fremont than for tech companies to migrate to bigger cities.

Fremont can't support high-tech companies!

Actually, it has been for over 10 years. Sycamore Education, the anchor tenant of the Forward Fremont project, has been in Fremont running an international, technology business for over a decade. Fremont is also home to two large, successful institutions of higher learning (Metro Community College and Midland University) that continue to feed young, energetic digital natives into our community.
